Who we Are

TrillaMed, founded by U.S. Army Airborne Ranger Veterans, is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business specializing in distributing medical and facility products. Our value-added approach combines expert support, logistics, and post-sale service to deliver efficient, mission-driven solutions.

Advancing Federal Healthcare

We serve over 700 federal healthcare facilities worldwide, including those operated by the Dept of Veterans Affairs, Dept of Defense, National Institutes of Health, and Bureau of Indian Affairs..

World-Class OEMs

We source products exclusively from industry-leading Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs), such as GE HealthCare and Stryker, ensuring the highest quality standards.

Beyond the Transaction

We use a value-added approach to ensure federal healthcare partners receive not just products, but comprehensive, mission-focused solutions that enhance care and efficiency.
